FAQ

How does LearnEmp work?

LearnEmp is a social crowdfunding platform for students who cannot afford a worthy education in a required university or course. LearnEmp enables donors to check a student's potential and make a contribution in order to help and empower the student.

Will the donor be paid back?

The donor is not paid back. LearnEmp works on the principle of 'Pay it Forward Loans'.
When a donor makes a contribution towards a student in need, he/she will automatically oblige the student in question to make a contribution in the future to fund and help another student in need. It's all about ensuring a better future delivered by the promising students on roll.

How much should I donate?

When you have browsed through our list of students in need of funding, you can pick any-one and donate any amount you prefer. The minimum amountis 5$. On every student's page you can view the funding required and how much other donors have already donated. You will receive a letter of gratitude from the student and LearnEmp for your contribution.
