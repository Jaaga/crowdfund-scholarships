DONATION

Our system is simple. We select candidates for a scholarship based on preset criterias by our team, where we consider but not limit it to economic background, academic performance and extra curricular skills.



STUDENT BENEFITS FROM YOUR LOAN

You as a donor choose the candidate you would like to sponsor.The student utilizes the loan for paying his/her academic fee and other basic necessities.



STUDENT PAYS IT FORWARD

The student who has been sponsored through our system gets inducted into an honour-based pay-it-forward process where the student once he graduates and starts earning gets an option to sponsor another deserving student.
