STUDENT BENEFITS FROM YOUR LOAN

You as a donor choose the candidate you would like to sponsor.The student utilizes the loan for paying his/her academic fee and other basic necessities.




STUDENT PAYS IT FORWARD

The student who has been sponsored through our system gets inducted into an honour-based pay-it-forward process where the student once graduated and starts earning, gets an option to sponsor another student.


FAQ

How does LearnEmp work?

LearnEmp is a social crowdfunding platform for students who cannot afford higher education in a required university or course.LearnEmp enables donor to check a student's potential and accomplishments and make a contribution in order to help and empower the student.

Will the donor be paid back?

The donor is not paid back. LearnEmp works on the principle of 'Pay it Forward Loans'.When a donor makes a contribution towards a student in need, he/she will automatically oblige the student in question to make a contribution in the future to fund and help another student in need. It's all about ensuring a better future delivered by the promising students on roll.

How much should I donate?

When you have browsed through our list of students in need of funding, you can pick any-one and donate any amount you prefer. The minimum amount is $5 (Rs.300*). On every student's page you can view the funding required and how much the student has been already funded. You will receive a letter of gratitude from the student and LearnEmp for your contribution.

About Us

The Team

Founded in January 2014,LearEmp comprises of eight individuals from across India who are passionate about learning and sharing knowledge and skills. The team was brought together by Jaaga Study, an initiative by Mr.Freeman Murray. The team strives to level the play field by providing opportunities to students who do not have the means to pursue higher education due to lack of funding.
